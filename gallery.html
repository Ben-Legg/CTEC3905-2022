Care Indexes

We have created a custom sunlight and water index, to show how demanding each plant is to grow, and listed something unique/interesting about that variety of plant.

- Low Light

- Indirect Light

- Bright Light

- Drought Resistant

- Let Soil Dry Between Waters

- Moist Soil

Each index gives a rough idea of how how much of each of these resources a plant demands. Plants with a 4/5 on either scale typically require the most of this resource, while plants with 1/2 need very little.

If you are looking for a variety of indoor plant that is easy to care for and won't die on you while you're away, we recommend choosing a variety with a low water index. Choosing a plant that likes lots of sun isn't usually a problem, as long as you water it as recommended and leave it in a spot that recieves lots of daylight!
